With over 8 years of experience planning and executing weddings, private, and corporate events, we are passionate about delivering a seamless, stress-free and beautifully crafted experience. We pride ourselves on our innovative and distinctive style, calming presence, and immaculate attention to detail.

Laura Burchfield Events is proud to have established relationships with the best vendors in the industry and we will work closely with them to orchestrate all design and logistical elements throughout the entire planning process. We highly value the unique relationships we forge with each of our clients and customize our process accordingly, knowing there is no single formula to event planning or design.

We understand that emotions run high throughout the planning process and we will help you navigate through it all so you're able to truly enjoy this special time. We cannot wait to meet you and bring your vision to life!


a little about the founder...



Laura feels that event planning perfectly suits her meticulous yet fun loving personality. No matter the size or scale of the event, she approaches each event with her signature creativity and style. She believes bringing together artful design, strategic coordination, and expert communication is the magic behind a successful event.

Her many years in the industry have contributed to her style of warm sophistication with a modern sensibility. Over the years Laura has planned and designed events of all shapes and sizes from 30 to 2,500 guests with no detail forgotten. She found her true calling 2 years ago when she started working with those getting married and planning their weddings.
