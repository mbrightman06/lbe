With over 8 years of experience planning and executing weddings, private and corporate events, we are passionate about innovation, thoroughness and about delivering extraordinary results.

Laura Burchfield Events will work closely with our trusted and talented vendors to bring your vision to life by orchestrating all design and logistical elements throughout the entire planning process. We pride ourselves in our attention to detail, creativity, and calming presence.

Our value lies in our level of experience, attention to detail and distinctive style. We understand that throughout the planning process emotions run high and we will help you navigate through an emotional time. We value the relationships we forge with our clients knowing there is no single formula to event planning or design. We will customize our process for each individual client.

Our mission is to create events that are seamless, stress-free and beautifully crafted. We are proud to have established relationships with the best vendors in the industry and, together, we will collaboratively bring your vision to life.

A LITTLE ABOUT THE FOUNDER...



Laura feels that event planning perfectly suits her meticulous yet fun loving personality. No matter the size or scale of the event, she approaches each event with her signature creativity and style. She believes bringing together artful design, strategic coordination, and expert communication is the magic behind a successful event.

Her many years in the industry have contributed to her style of warm sophistication with a modern sensibility. Over the years Laura has planned and designed events of all shapes and sizes from 30 to 2,500 guests with no detail forgotten. She found her true calling 2 years ago when she started working with those getting married and planning their weddings.
