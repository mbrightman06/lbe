day of coordinating

You have dreamt about planning your wedding since as long as you can remember so taking care of booking all of your vendors and designing every last piece is right in your wheel house. Now all you need is someone to run the show. The Day-of Coordinating Package is exactly what you need. Don’t worry...we don’t just show up the “Day of”. We are here for advice along the way with coordination of vendors, timeline creation, rehearsal direction and wedding day management. We will handle all of the logistics so that you, your family and your friends can relax and enjoy the best day of your lives.

package includes

Unlimited email communication for constant contact between client and planner to ensure everyone is on the same page

Vendor contract review

1 (one) onsite meeting with the couple as needed for vendor walk through and/or layout logistics

Access to our preferred vendor list. We will connect you with the vendors that we recommend for any outstanding needs that you have

Create logistical layouts and floor plans to send to the venue and vendors

Wedding weekend timeline and itinerary- Leave the logistics and details to us. We will share this with vendors, wedding party, venue and everyone involved in the event activities

Manage and supervise schedules of vendors for load in, set up, breaks/meals, break down and load out

Coordinate the wedding party and family through the ceremony rehearsal

Day of Coordination (up to 10 hours) acting as the point person for the day of the wedding accompanied by assistant and staff as needed. Hours can be extended upon request

Assist photographer in coordinating members needed for photos

Assist in final reception details with set up and break down of guest book, favors, escort cards, signage, etc.

Hold and distribute final payments and vendor gratuities at the event's conclusion

Pack all personal items and deliver to designated person to take from the venue
